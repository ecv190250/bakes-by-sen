There is nothing quite like the smell of fresh cookies wafting through the house. Today, I am sharing my go-to recipe for the ultimate chocolate chip cookies. These are the perfect balance of a set edge and a soft, chewy center.

**Yields:** 12 cookies **Prep time:** 15 minutes **Bake time:** 10 minutes

### Ingredients

- **226g** Butter, softened
- **80g** Cocoa Powder
- **80g** Granulated Sugar
- **80g** Light Brown Sugar
- **2** Large Eggs
- **2 tsp** Vanilla Extract
- **2 cups** All-Purpose Flour
- **1 tsp** Baking Powder
- **½ tsp** Salt
- **½ cup** Chocolate Chips

### Instructions

1. **Cream the Base:** Combine the cocoa powder and softened butter in a bowl. Mix for two minutes until the texture is smooth and well-incorporated.
2. **Add Sweetness & Eggs:** Add both the brown sugar and granulated sugar, along with the eggs. Beat the mixture for another two minutes to ensure it’s light and combined.
3. **Combine Dry Ingredients:** Add the flour, baking powder, salt, and vanilla extract. Mix for an additional two minutes.
4. **The Best Part:** Fold in your chocolate chips until they are evenly distributed through the dough.
5. **Prep for Baking:** Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
6. **Pro-Tip for Consistency:** To achieve bakery-quality, uniform cookies, use your kitchen scale! With this recipe, each cookie should weigh approximately **77 grams**. Portion your dough into 12 equal balls—this ensures that every single cookie bakes at the exact same rate for perfect results every time.
7. **Bake:** Place your dough balls on a baking sheet and bake for approximately 10 minutes. Keep a close eye on them—you want the edges to be set, but the center to remain soft!

Let these cool slightly before digging in. There is nothing better than a warm cookie with a glass of milk.

**Happy Baking!**
