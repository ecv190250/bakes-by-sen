Before the rush of the workday kicks in, I take a few minutes to build something good for myself. It’s not about perfection; it’s about starting the day with intention.

This isn't a complex recipe—it's my way of turning a simple breakfast into a moment of calm.

### The Components

- **The Base:** A soft, glazed donut (the perfect sweet foundation).
- **The Cream:** Whipped cream cheese, lightened up to provide a tangy, velvety contrast to the sweet donut.
- **The Toppings:**
    - **Fresh Fruit:** A mix of fresh strawberries, blackberries, and raspberries for brightness and acidity.
    - **The Indulgence:** A drizzle of Kinder chocolate and Belgian chocolate, swirled together.
- **The Essential:** A hot, fresh cup of coffee.

### The Assembly (The Routine)

1. **Prep the Plate:** Place your donut in a glass container (or your favorite shallow bowl).
2. **Add the Cream:** Dollop the whipped cream cheese generously over the top.
3. **The Art:** Arrange your fresh berries. I like to keep them vibrant and centered.
4. **The Finish:** Drizzle the melted Kinder and Belgian chocolate in a swirling motion over the cream cheese and berries.
5. **Pairing:** Serve immediately alongside your favorite coffee.

### Why It Works for a Work Morning

### The Routine

I don't overthink it. I set the donut, add the whipped cream cheese, arrange the fresh berries, and finish with a quick drizzle of chocolate. It takes less time than it looks, but it completely changes the energy of my morning.

### Why It Matters

It’s not just the sugar rush; it’s the visual pleasure. Taking the time to plate a meal this beautifully—even if it’s just a donut with some fruit—transforms breakfast from a chore into a moment of calm. It’s the small, beautiful things that give me the energy to take on the day.

Eating well, even in small and simple ways, is how I fuel myself before heading out. It’s a quiet, creative habit that keeps me grounded.
